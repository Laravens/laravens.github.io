Welcome to the community

We strive to make the Web a greater place

We are Laravens. Living in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, we found that the developer communities here though overflowing with potential were scattered. With the drive to change that, we started by uniting devs under the framework we love dearly, Laravel! We started having monthly talks called Laratalks and are planning a number of other things in order to make the dev communities more condusive and a fun place for everybody. Follow us on Facebook, where we're most active!
